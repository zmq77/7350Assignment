    BELIEVING

    Huang Xuan dances Chaoshan "YingGe Dance"

    The gongs and drums sounded, the firecrackers went off, and the Yingge teenagers set up their formation. The clothes are fluttering, the arms are waving, and the New Year in the Year of the Dragon has arrived.

    On the walls that have been mottled by the sun and water over the years, red Spring Festival couplets with the characters "Fu" are hung. Huang Xuan picked up his pen and wrote a sentence: Only after listening to the wind eight hundred times can you realize that it is the human world.

    Why is this sentence, and how to understand it? Huang Xuan replied, we need time to listen and observe, and then you can gain insight into the most real, sincere and ultimate world.
